About us

Meet Dr. Siva Kalyan Reddy

Dr. Siva Kalyan Reddy is a qualified Physiotherapist (PT) with hands-on expertise in musculoskeletal rehabilitation, sports injury management, and pain relief therapies. His calm, patient-first approach ensures every individual receives a personalised recovery plan.

Dedicated to evidence-based physiotherapy, Dr. Kalyan works closely with patients across all age groups — from athletes recovering from injuries to elderly patients regaining mobility and independence.

Our services

Comprehensive physiotherapy services designed to address a wide range of conditions and help you recover faster.

🏃

Sports injury rehab

ACL tears, sprains, strains, and performance recovery programs for athletes at all levels.

🦴

Post-surgery rehab

Structured recovery plans following joint replacements, spinal surgery, or orthopaedic procedures.

🔄

Back & neck pain

Evidence-based treatment for chronic and acute spinal conditions, postural correction, and disc issues.

🧠

Neurological rehab

Rehabilitation support for stroke recovery, Parkinson's disease, and balance disorders.

Manual therapy

Elderly care

Fall prevention, mobility programs, and strength training tailored for older adults.
